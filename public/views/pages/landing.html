DeFi

Decentralized finance, or DeFi, is traditional finance combined with the decentralized nature of blockchain technology. Why is decentralization important? The answer; sovereignty, security, and privacy. With DeFi, there are no intermediaries or institutions involved- meaning when you own a digital asset, you have 100% control.
